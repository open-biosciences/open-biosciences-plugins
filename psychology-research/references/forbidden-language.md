# Forbidden Language

Lexical filter wordlist read by the forbidden-language validator at the publish gate. Matches are case-insensitive and word-boundary-aware. The filter currently runs at WARN severity (advisory).

## Superlatives that overclaim provider quality

- premier
- elite
- extraordinarily
- formidable
- exceptional
- unparalleled
- masterful
- world-class
- top-rated
- best-in-class

## Treatment claims that overstate evidence

- gold standard
- the standard treatment
- proven cure
- guaranteed results
- definitive treatment
- groundbreaking

## Clinical-rank language to avoid

- best clinician
- top therapist
- most qualified
- ultimate expert

## Note

Phrases on this list may appear legitimately in quoted source material (e.g., quoting a credentialing body's own language). The filter flags occurrences in the report's narrative; reviewer judgment determines whether each hit is in narrative voice or quotation. The filter does not currently distinguish quoted from narrative use; that's a Tier-N improvement.
